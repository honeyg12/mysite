A short story behind StudyPMA

" My parent asked me to give PMA test as they wished that their son join Pakistan army. But at that time I was not prepared. I was not enough mature to join army. But I did that at the words of My parents. As I don't want to refuse their words. But i can't prepared anything and I have no idea what sort of test I am going to face. I have no material to study. Although I have one book but it couldn't help as much. I have visit some of the websites but there is a lot of mess on these websites and it's hard to get the actual thing. As a result I Failed. I failed for the first time in my life. I have got 87% marks in my matriculation and 73% in intermediate, and i failed the initial test.

So I figured out what is the main reason behind it. And as I have said I can't find the target material to study, so it happened. At that time I think this is happening with lot of the student's like me. They are not getting the right material. And may be they also fail.

At that day I took oath that I would resolve this issue not just for me but for the every student who is willing to crack PMA exam, who heartly loves army and who want to learn new things. So, at that time I knew little bit of coding and computer skills but I've to work and learn hard to make it perfect. So I learned a lot. Work 3 straight months without a leave and that time was tough.Very tough. But i want to resolve this issue. So, i made this website all by myself and make it free from ads from money and more from everything I make it easy to use so anybody from any part of Pakistan understand it and learn from it. So if you learn everthing from it just say thanks from you heart and crack the exma. This platform is for you, use it, learn from it, and share it with your friends and family member's and anybody who wants to learnPMA. Thanks! "
